## What is RankEarly?

Most SEO tools leave you in a dashboard. RankEarly is built for a more autonomous workflow — it helps you know what to write from real search data, then AI carries the work forward by researching, drafting, assessing, and polishing the content.

If you are starting from scratch or working with thin traffic data, RankEarly helps you make decisions before you waste weeks on the wrong content.

RankEarly ships as a web platform and an agent skills bundle you can install in Claude, Codex, OpenClaw, or any MCP-compatible agent.

## How the workflow fits together

<Steps>
  <Step title="Set up your project">
    Add your site or product URL. RankEarly analyzes the business, identifies competitors, and saves project context such as audience, features, goals, and seed phrases.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Build a keyword library">
    Run [Keyword Expansion](/research/keyword-expansion) to create or extend a project keyword library. This is usually the first step before Primary terms and Topic clusters.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Run the right research">
    Use [Primary terms](/research/primary-term) for positioning, [Topic clusters](/research/topic-clusters) for content planning, or [SERP gap analysis](/research/serp-gap-analysis) to validate whether a topic is worth pursuing.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Turn winners into content">
    Launch [Content Brief](/planning/content-brief) from a subtopic or keyword. If you use agent skills, the brief flows directly into research, drafting, assessment, and polishing.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Who is it for?

RankEarly is for people who want guided workflows instead of a keyword dashboard:

- **Founders and solo marketers** building content without a full SEO team
- **Lean teams** that need a plan, not just keyword exports
- **New or low-authority sites** where Google Search Console data is thin or nonexistent
- **AI-agent users** who want SEO research and writing inside the tools they already use

RankEarly is not built for enterprise SEO teams that need mature reporting, GSC integrations, or large agency workflows. It is optimized for people who need better content decisions early.
