## What is RankEarly?

Most SEO tools need months of traffic data to be useful. RankEarly is built for cold-start projects, when you have little or no traffic but still need to decide what to write.

Instead of guessing at topics and wasting weeks on content that will not rank, you validate your decisions upfront with keyword data, real search results, and AI analysis.

RankEarly gives you four research modes plus one downstream brief workflow:

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Keyword expansion" icon="expand" href="/research/keyword-expansion">
    Turn one idea into 100 to 500 keywords, then save them to your keyword library for later research.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Primary terms" icon="crosshairs" href="/research/primary-term">
    Use your keyword library to find winnable terms for positioning, messaging, and product communication.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Content angles" icon="arrows-split-up-and-left" href="/research/content-angles">
    Turn one idea into 5 winnable content angles when you know the topic but need the best framing.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Topic clusters" icon="diagram-project" href="/research/topic-clusters">
    Turn a keyword library into content pillars and subtopics you can prioritize and brief later.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Content brief" icon="file-pen" href="/planning/content-brief">
    Turn a validated keyword or subtopic into a step-by-step brief with intent, format, must-includes, titles, and outline.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

Each workflow can stand alone, but they work best together when your strategy is evolving.

## How the workflow fits together

<Steps>
  <Step title="Set up your project">
    Add your site or product URL. RankEarly analyzes the business, identifies competitors, and saves project context such as audience, features, goals, and seed phrases.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Build a keyword library">
    Run [Keyword Expansion](/research/keyword-expansion) to create or extend a project keyword library. This is usually the first step before Primary terms and Topic clusters.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Run the right research">
    Use [Primary terms](/research/primary-term) for positioning, [Topic clusters](/research/topic-clusters) for content planning, or [Content angles](/research/content-angles) when you already have one idea and need the best angle.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Turn winners into briefs">
    Launch [Content Brief](/planning/content-brief) from a subtopic, support query, or keyword when you are ready to turn research into a draftable plan.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Who is it for?

RankEarly is for builders who want a guided content strategy workflow instead of a keyword dashboard:

- **Early-stage startup founders** building content-led growth
- **Indie hackers and solo entrepreneurs** who need a plan, not just keyword exports
- **New or low-authority sites** where Google Search Console data is thin or nonexistent

RankEarly is not built for enterprise SEO teams that need mature reporting, GSC integrations, or large agency workflows. It is optimized for new-site builders who need to make better decisions early.
