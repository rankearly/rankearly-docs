## What is RankEarly?

Most SEO tools need months of traffic data to be useful. RankEarly is built for cold-start — when you have zero traffic but need to decide what to write.

Instead of guessing at topics and wasting weeks on content that won't rank, you research and validate content decisions upfront with real search data.

RankEarly helps you answer **"what should I write?"** through four research modes:

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Primary term" icon="crosshairs" href="/research/primary-term">
    Find winnable terms for positioning and product communication. Use them in landing pages, taglines, and feature descriptions.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Topic clusters" icon="diagram-project" href="/research/topic-clusters">
    Surface content ideas you haven't thought of yet. Get up to 10 content pillars, each with subtopics ready to write.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Content angles" icon="arrows-split-up-and-left" href="/research/content-angles">
    Turn one idea into 5 winnable content angles. You know the topic — find the best way to approach it.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Keyword expansion" icon="expand" href="/research/keyword-expansion">
    Turn one idea into 300-500 keywords for bulk research and pSEO planning.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

Each mode works standalone or together. Run any of them multiple times as your strategy evolves.

## Who is it for?

RankEarly is for builders who want to grow organic traffic without needing years of SEO expertise:

- **Early-stage startup founders** building content-led growth
- **Indie hackers and solo entrepreneurs** who need a content plan, not a keyword dashboard
- **New or low-authority sites** where Google Search Console data doesn't exist yet

## How it works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Set up your project">
    Enter your website URL. RankEarly analyzes your site and identifies competitors, target audience, and seed phrases automatically. You review and refine.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Run research">
    Pick a research mode based on where you are. Looking for your core terms? Start with Primary Term. Need a full content plan? Run Topic Clusters.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review winnability">
    Every term and topic gets a winnability verdict — **Build**, **Challenging**, or **Skip** — based on SERP gap analysis. Focus your effort on what you can actually rank for.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save and plan">
    Bookmark the terms, pillars, and angles that matter. Build your content strategy from validated research, not guesswork.
  </Step>
</Steps>
