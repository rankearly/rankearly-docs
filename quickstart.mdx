## Start with a project

Every workflow in RankEarly starts with a project. It gives RankEarly the basic context it needs to keep the research relevant.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create a new project">
    Open your [dashboard](https://rankearly.pro/project) and click **New Project**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add your URL">
    Paste your homepage, product page, or landing page. It does not have to be a root domain.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review what RankEarly finds">
    RankEarly identifies your product, audience, competitors, and business goals. Fix anything that looks off, then save.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Run your first research

If you are starting from scratch, begin with [Keyword Expansion](/research/keyword-expansion). It gives you a clearer picture of how people search around one idea and makes the next steps more useful.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Keyword Expansion">
    From your project, start a new **Keyword Expansion** run.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Describe one idea">
    Add a feature, topic, or customer question you want to explore. Choose where to save the results in your project.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review the results">
    Use the results to see how people search for that idea. If the output looks useful, keep building from it.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## A simple first path

If you just want the shortest route through the product:

1. Create a project
2. Run [Keyword Expansion](/research/keyword-expansion)
3. Open [Topic Clusters](/research/topic-clusters)
4. Move the best keyword or subtopic into [Content Brief](/planning/content-brief)
